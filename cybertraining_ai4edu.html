AI4EDU: Cloud Infrastructure-Enabled Training for AI in Educational Research and Assessment

Overall Objective

Develop the AI4EDU platform with training modules, data analysis and cloud management tools, sample projects with curated data sets, and a depository for community resource sharing.

Long-term Goal

Support the training of educational researchers on AI/machine learning with cloud computing involving large educational datasets; develop a comprehensive curriculum to improve educational research.

Project Impact

Support the AI for Education initiatives, by bridging the gap between the analytical techniques taught in the classroom, with the tools and skillsets needed to work with Big Data in education.

Training Modules

• Introduction to virtualization
• Introduction to cloud computing
• Introduction to AWS
• More training modules to be developed...
